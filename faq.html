FAQ - Crypto Minning investment LTD

FAQ

About Company

In this section, you can find answers to the questions that concern the project participants most often. If you can’t find an answer to your question, please contact our Technical Support.

How to become a project investor?

To start cooperation with our Company, you shouldn’t visit our office, because we have simplified the investment process to the maximum. You just need to pass the registration on the Fund’s website.

What information shall I provide upon registration?

We respect the intent of our clients to remain anonymous, therefore we do not require passport data. You shall only provide a minimum amount of information that allows for your login identification.

How many accounts can I have in the project?

The project rules prohibit multiple registrations and each participant can have only one registered account. In case our Security Department becomes aware of additional accounts, they will block them with the deposited funds.

What do investments in the project suggest?

After registration on the website, you will gain access to your personal account, by means of which you can deposit. Your funds will be utilized for the Company’s operations, whereas no additional actions from your side are required.

What are the business hours of the Company?

We work 24/7 and provide participants with round-the-clock access to the investment process.

How to create a deposit?

Investments in the project are voluntary, and each participant determines the amount of deposit on his/her own. You can also choose any type of depositing from those provided by the project.

Within what period will the funds be credited to my account in the project?

As a rule, funds crediting to the account is performed immediately but the time of money receipt depends on the payment system chosen by you. Please note that there may be delays caused by force majeure from the side of payment systems.

Are there any limits on the minimum and maximum deposit amount?

Under our marketing terms, you can deposit an amount of $10 to $100,000.

How often will I get a profit?

Profit will be calculated in accordance with the investment proposal of the project, you can find it in the relevant section of our resource.

What payment systems can I use to credit funds to my account?

To ensure comfort investment conditions, we have linked up several popular payment vehicles. You can make a deposit by means of Perfect Money, Payeer, Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Dogecoin, BitcoinCash.

Will the profit be credited directly to my purse in the payment system?

For security purposes, the funds will be credited to your account, from where you can withdraw profit to your purse in the payment system.

When will the profit received in the project be available for withdrawal?

You can withdraw your profit at any time, the amount of the minimum request is $0.1.

How long does it take to process a withdrawal request?

Profit withdrawal is carried out manually and it can take up to 48 hours. At the same time, we try to process received requests in the quickest possible time.

Is it possible to withdraw profit to any payment system?

You can withdraw your funds to the payment system from which they were deposited.

Are there any fees or hidden payments in the project?

We do not charge any fees and hidden payments on deposited and withdrawn funds.

What is an affiliate program?

The affiliate program is a reward plan for participants attracting new investors to the project. We are interested in the active growth of the number of company clients, therefore we pay a profit of 5% of the deposits made by participants attracted by you.

Who can become a member of the affiliate program in the project?

Any registered participant can become our partner. Immediately after account activation, you will receive a unique affiliate link that you will use to attract new members to the project.

Is it obligatory to have a personal deposit in the system to make a profit from the attracted investments?

It is not necessary to have a personal deposit to participate in the affiliate program. You can use its advantages as an opportunity to make a profit without investments.

Is it safe to use the project website?

Our investment resource is equipped with advanced methods of protection against DDoS-attacks and reliable data encryption. You can be completely sure that cooperation with the project is safe.

Does the Company share its clients’ data with third parties?

We do not share our clients’ data with third parties and work on the principles of anonymity and confidentiality of personal information.

If I lost access to my account, what should I do?

If for whatever reason, you have lost your account password, we recommend using the password recovery form.

If I face difficulties in the process of investment, who should I contact?

The Technical Support of the Company will give you a competent answer to any question related to the work of the project and the investment process.

If I face difficulties in the process of investment, who should I contact?

The Technical Support of the Company will give you a competent answer to any question related to the work of the project and the investment process.

Within what jurisdiction does the company operate? How can I review the regulatory documents of the Company?

The documents are available for review on this website. The Company is registered in the UK.

What should I do if I have not found the answer to my question?

Our experts from the Technical Support will be glad to answer any of your questions.
